About Us

Introduction

Hi5 Electrical Ltd is a trusted electrical service provider delivering high-quality solutions across the residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. We specialise in electrical installation, maintenance, energy solutions, and smart automation, with a strong focus on safety, reliability, and quality workmanship.

Our director is also a director of Star Fang Electrical, an approved ICT contractor for the Ministry of Education New Zealand, bringing extensive experience in education sector projects and large-scale infrastructure delivery.

Mission

  • Delivering safe, reliable, and high-quality electrical solutions
  • Providing exceptional service with attention to detail
  • Building long-term relationships based on trust and performance

Vision

  • To become a leading provider of electrical and infrastructure solutions
  • To drive innovation, efficiency, and sustainability in every project
  • To exceed client expectations through quality and professionalism
